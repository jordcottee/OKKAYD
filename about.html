Our Mission

Revolutionise Legal Operations with AI

At Okkayd, we believe that modern legal and business teams deserve a smarter way to work. We are a team of legal tech enthusiasts, AI experts, and product designers united by a single mission: to create a platform that makes contract management intuitive, efficient, and secure. Legal work is demanding and detail-oriented, but much of it is also repetitive. We founded Okkayd to build an intelligent solution that automates the time-consuming tasks of contract review and analysis. By leveraging the latest advancements in AI, we have created a platform that not only saves time but also significantly reduces risk by instantly highlighting potential issues, missing clauses, and non-compliance. We are committed to building a product that is not just powerful, but also accessible. Our clean, user-friendly interface ensures that every member of your team, from legal professionals to sales and procurement, can confidently manage contracts. We prioritise security and data privacy above all else, ensuring your sensitive legal documents are protected at every step. Join us on our journey to transform legal operations and empower your team to focus on strategy, not paperwork.
